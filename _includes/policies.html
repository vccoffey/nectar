Policies

Cost

$25–35 sliding scale — pay at the door. Cash or Venmo @Nectar-Fusion.

We offer and encourage work trades in exchange for free entry for those who want to be part of the event or for whom cost is a barrier. NOTAFLOF — no one turned away for lack of funds.

Health

We care deeply about the health of our community. Please use your best judgment — do not come if you are feeling sick, know you could be carrying something transmittable, or have symptoms of something contagious. We encourage you to take a covid test before attending.

If you see someone dancing with a mask on and would like to dance with them, please check in to see if they would feel more comfortable if you were also wearing a mask.

Consent

We practice radical, enthusiastic consent. Check in before dancing with someone. Respect a no. Take care of each other.
