About

Nectar Fusion Playground is a queer-led, culture-shaping space to explore creative expression and connection through the improvisational form of fusion partner dance, and beyond.

Here, we invite:

  • Dancing together free of normative gender roles — you do you
  • Honoring the function of form — come take a class, be mindful of our bodies in space
  • Challenging ourselves from whatever level we're at — all are welcome
  • Prioritizing attunement, play, and relating over transacting
  • Taking care of ourselves and each other
  • Practicing radical, enthusiastic consent
  • Co-creating an inclusive and courageous environment to be real in
  • Getting juiced so that we can continue to do the hard work of building a better world

This is a BIPOC and LGBTQIA2S+ friendly space. No racism, bigotry, or hateful speech or actions will be tolerated.
